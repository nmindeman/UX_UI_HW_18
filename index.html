My UX Skills

User Research

I conduct thorough user research to understand the needs and pain points of the target audience, ensuring that the product is designed with the user in mind.

Wireframing

I create detailed wireframes that serve as blueprints for the final product, allowing for iterative design improvements before moving to high-fidelity prototypes.

Prototyping

I build interactive prototypes that demonstrate the flow and functionality of the design, enabling stakeholders to experience the product before development begins.
